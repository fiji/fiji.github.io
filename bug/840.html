Inactivation of SCIFIO returns import speed to normal
Comment 1 Curtis Rueden 2014-07-07 16:52:53 CDT 
SCIFIO supports a wider variety of TIFF files than ImageJ 1.x does, but is currently slower than ImageJ1's TIFF reader. Turning off SCIFIO support is the best option for now until the SCIFIO TIFF reader gets faster.

This issue is tracked at: https://github.com/scifio/scifio/issues/7
