Comment 1 Johannes Schindelin 2014-03-05 15:40:02 CST 
Please understand that the modern mode is only a preview at the moment (as I made quite clear in my announcement mail a couple of months back).

Therefore, I am not surprised that using a legacy plugin interacting with the modern GUI does not quite work yet... we focus on the image processing plugins first, and before they work correctly, I will probably put less emphasis on fixing legacy plugins unrelated to processing images.

And no, the "Fiji won't quit" (unless this is a backhanded comment at our dedication to keep going with this underfunded project) is not a problem I am aware of... I thought we had nailed that. Please feel free to file another bug report for that, with a macro reproducing the error.
