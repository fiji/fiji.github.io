Comment 1 Mark Hiner 2015-08-11 08:36:55 CDT 
It looks like you manually installed the QuickTime plugins[1]. These plugins are not compatible with 64-bit java[2] which you are using.

If you remove the QuickTime plugins are you still unable to open your dataset? If so, can you try the Bio-Formats importer[3]?
