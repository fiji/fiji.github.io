* Can you please test with a Fiji installation that has no extra update sites enabled? If it works, then try enabling your desired update sites one by one until you find the one which breaks things.

If the 3D Viewer works, and the problem still happens with a freshly downloaded Fiji without extra update sites, then please post the exact exception you are seeing, along with the exact steps you took to generate it.
