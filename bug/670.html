This bug in the Process>Batch>Macro command is fixed in the ImageJ 1.48e8 daily build, available at

   https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/2810011/ij.jar

The daily build is not available via Help>Update ImageJ due to the U.S. Government partial shutdown.

The fix required adding

   if (imp==null) imp=Opener.openUsingBioFormats(path);

just after

   imp = IJ.openImage(path);

in the BatchProcessor.processFolder() method.
