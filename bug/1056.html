Comment 1 Mark Hiner 2015-04-16 10:57:20 CDT 
Thanks for uploading sample data. It does look like there is a problem with your OME-TIFF - even with an up-to-date Fiji and Bio-Formats 5.1.

The Bio-Formats team has their own bug reporting system, so unfortunately you'll have to contact them directly to look into this issue. Instructions can be found here:

http://www.openmicroscopy.org/site/support/bio-formats5.1/about/bug-reporting.html

Thank you, also, for the full error message from bug reporting in Fiji. I filed an issue to track it: https://github.com/fiji/Bug_Submitter/issues/4
