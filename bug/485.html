Comment 1 Johannes Schindelin 2012-09-06 23:41:31 CDT 
There was a bug in the updater which -- unfortunately -- prevents certain versions of the updater from updating the updater correctly. However, I tested installing Fiji from scratch and that one is still able to update correctly.

One of the following two methods should get you back to a working system, though:

1) delete Fiji.app/jars/ij-ui-swing-updater-2.0.0-SNAPSHOT.jar and restart Fiji

2) download http://jenkins.imagej.net/job/Stable-Fiji/ws/Fiji.app/plugins/Fiji_Updater.jar and drag & drop it onto the Fiji main window (or if that window does not even appear, overwrite Fiji.app/plugins/Fiji_Updater.jar) and try again.
Comment 2 Johannes Schindelin 2012-09-12 22:07:11 CDT 
I am assuming that one of the suggested actions worked.
